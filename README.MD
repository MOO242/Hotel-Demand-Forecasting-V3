
# Calculate  

RevPAR = Occupancy Rate × ADR (Average Daily Rate). The single most important hotel KPI.
TotalRevPAR = Total revenue (not just rooms) per available room
ADR = Average Daily Rate (you already worked with this — good!)
GOPPAR = Gross Operating Profit Per Available Room

Distribution & Digital

Digital Mix = % of bookings coming from digital channels vs. offline/voice
Channels = Direct (hotel website), OTA (Booking.com, Expedia), GDS (corporate travel), Voice
Segmentation = Transient (individual), Group, Contract/Corporate; Leisure vs. Business
